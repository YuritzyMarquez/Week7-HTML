About Aphelios

Aphelios is an ADC (Attack Damage Carry). He is mainly played in bot along with a support. He is known to be one of the most 'broken' champions when played right. Aphelios was released in 2019. He is also known in his lore as "The Weapon of the Faithful."

Aphelios' Abilities

Aphelios has 4 abilities like all champions, but also has a passive. His passive is "The Hitman and the Seer," which allows him to wield 5 lunar weapons. His first ability is "Weapon Abilities," his second is "Phase," his third is "Weapon Queue System," and his last one is "Moonlight Vigil." His last ability is also his ultimate which is unlocked at level 6 and is stronger then the rest of his abilities.

About Viego

Viego is known as the Runed King. He is in search of ways to resurrect his queen and will stop at nothing to achieve it and will sacrifice everyone for it. He is a Jungler. He doesn't have a specific lane like top, mid, or bot laners do. He roams in the jungle and secures objectives like drakes, baron, and rift herald. Securing these objectives will help his team defeat the enemy team to the nexus. Veigo can also gank lanes and secure kills for his team, which will give them a gold and item advantage against the enemy team, thus making them stronger.

Viego's Abilities

Veigo's passive is "Sovereign's Domination," which allows him to possess champions he helps kill and heal a portion of his health. His first ability is "Blade of the Ruined King," his second ability is "Spectral Maw," his third ability is "Harrowed Path," and his last ability (his ultimate) is "Heartbreaker."

About Graves

Graves is know to be an outlaw. He is a bounty hunter along with his partner Twist Fate. Graves' original role is as a jungler, but is often seen being played as an ADC, mid laner, and top laner. He has a two barrowed shotgun and needs to reload every two autos.

Graves Abilities

Graves' passive is "New Destiny," this will push back any target that he aims at. His first ability is "End of the Line," his second ability is "Smoke Screen," his third ability is "Quickdraw," and his fourth ability (his ultimate) is "Collateral Damage."
